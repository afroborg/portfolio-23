## Information about Lund and TLTH for new students

An open-source effort to make it easier for new students to find information about Lund and TLTH. With content such as information about the city, the university, housing, and more, it's a great resource for new students. Whether you've just moved to Lund or are planning to move here, this is a great place to start.
